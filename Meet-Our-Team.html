Amar Waghmare

Chief Executive Officer (CEO)

Mr. Amar Waghmare, an engineer by academics, researcher by passion, business entrepreneur by heart with more than 25+ years of experience in the field of direct selling & financial sector, is a man of few words having the efficiency in customer mapping and creating sales funnel. He has established himself in multiple industries with a focus on sustainable growth through strong administrative and management skills to transform sales.

His passion towards Ayurveda has marked a niche for himself in the wellness Industry. He is more focused on his vision through lot of research on natural health beneficial fruits & herbs and bring out natural home bound chemical free products into the market. He encourages the local organic products that will give genuine benefits in this polluted environment.

Being the Managing Director of ATS, he strongly believes that mother nature has its own healing properties. He wants to bring change in the health sector through quality products that will enable people to have a sustainable health and wealth. Mr. Amar believes in Ethical Business Practice so EBP will be an important aspect to create the much needed value system in the Industry.
