Our Business Plan

Home - Our Business Plan

Do you want to experience wellness and start your journey going hand in hand with health and wealth? Then stop your search as you are at the right place. ATS is one of the fastest growing direct selling companies of India bringing the finest products of our mother nature to nurture our health with its unique opportunities that can lead to higher level of success.

The ATS products are offered at an attractive price that will not only enable us to save substantial amount of money on a weekly basis but also lead your business opportunities and sustainable career growth.

GET START YOUR CAREER IN WELLNESS & BE PART OF
A LEADING WELLNESS BUSINESS.

Associate with ATS in 2 different ways:

To do so, there is neither registration nor membership fees. Sign up with ATS is absolutely FREE.

1) Preferred Customer (PC): Any Indian citizen, irrespective of age or KYC compliance, can become a Preferred Customer (PC) of ATS for absolutely free without any registration fees or membership charges whatsoever. For this, simply register online and choose to buy ATS products of your own choice. Kindly note, there is no fixed product package nor minimum amount of mandatory purchase.

2) Independent Business Owner (IBO): Indian Citizen who has completed 18 years and above having their valid PAN (PERMANET ACCOUNT NUMBER) and KYC documents is entitled to be an IBO. Registration for the same is absolutely for FREE. You can voluntarily buy ATS products and activate your ids. Activation of your ids will entitle to earn and enjoy the weekly & monthly sales commissions and can achieve ATS business ranks, rewards, when you fulfil your sales targets/ sales volume criteria.

  • SHARE OUR PRODUCTS & UPGRADE YOUR BUSINESS VOLUMES
  • BOOST YOUR BUSINESS BY SHARING YOUR PRODUCT KNOWLEDGE & BUSINESS PLAN
  • BE SELF MOTIVATED TO MOTIVATE OTHERS

ATS IBO's Gets Benefits Like

  • Long life distributorship

  • No renewal fees
  • Training & Seminar free
  • Family protection plan
  • Domestic & International Trainings & Tours

PV (Point Value) Purchase: A PC can choose to purchase any ATS products of one’s own choice that carry PV (Point Value).

BV (Business Value) Purchase: A PC can choose to purchase products of one’s own choice (even a single product) directly that carry BV (Business Value) and start his/her exciting ATS business.

Joining Package

Joining Activation PV : 1000 PV

Retail Income

MRP - DP = RETAIL INCOME
2000 - 1500 = 500 (25%)

Example : If you sell a package worth Rs. 2000, you will get 25% on that package i.e Rs. 500. That is your Retail Income

Matching Income

20% of your both leg side matching.

First pair 2:1 and 1:2 then after 1:1.

Daily Capping : 2000/-

Example : Daily Rs. 2000 x 30 Days
= Rs. 60,000 (Per Month You Achieve)

Leadership Income

On Each and Every direct referral you will get 50% on your direct matching amount

Example:
If your Direct A earns Rs. 2000 then you will get 50% Leadership Income i.e Rs. 1000. If you have 5 directs and if each direct earns Rs. 2000 then you get 50% of each direct as a Leadership Income. Means you can get 5 x Rs. 1000 = Rs. 5,000 per day and Rs. 1,50,000 per month.

Note: 2 direct 1000 matching BV is mandatory

Self Repurchase Income

You will get 10% on your Self Purchase product BV amount.

Franchise Income

The Franchiser is required to tender deposit of minimum Rs 250000/- (Rupees Two Lakhs Fifty Thousand only) as Base stock inventory towards products of your choices.
