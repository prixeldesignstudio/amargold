Who We Are

ATS - a platform to transform your dreams into reality

AMAR GOLD TRADING & SERVICE is the brainchild of our director Mr. Amar Waghmare who has been the expertise in Agro & Ayurveda products. Though it’s just in its infancy with its inception in 2022, ATS aims in spreading the values of Ayurveda to reach out globally & work on the nutritional values to make a healthier nation along with his dedicated team.

This company is born out of the combination of passion and dedication with a deep commitment to offer advanced wellness solutions to aspiring people from all roads of life and convert their irregular 21st century lifestyle into a happy & holistic living.

What is Ayurvedic Wellness?

AYURVEDA is originated in India more than 5,000 years ago & is considered as the MOTHER OF ALL HEALING. It places great emphasis on prevention and encourages the maintenance of health through close attention to balance in one’s life, right thinking, diet, lifestyle and the use of herbs. Just as everyone has a unique fingerprint, each person has a particular pattern of energy- an individual combination of physical, mental and emotional characteristics which compromises their own constitution.

Currently around 6,50,000 doctors are practising this science in India with 70,000 doctors practising worldwide. Ayurveda advocates that each person is born with a basic genetic make-up called ‘Prakriti’. Maintaining prakriti is at the very core of what this healing system seeks to achieve. Ayurveda is a complete wellness system that encompasses all aspects of life, health and happiness. An important part of our prakriti is our ‘dosha’ or body type. There are three primary doshas or bio-energies known as Vata, Pitta and Kapha. Life Energy- Prana -was the foundational healing property of herbs. In this modern age Ayurveda got pushed out of its role & gradually replaced by Allopathic medicine. At the same time the health condition of Indian population too have deteriorated and become a major factor of the declining quality of life. It is based on the belief that health and wellness depend on a delicate balance between the mind, body and spirit.

WELLNESS is aiming for a complete wellbeing of people around us with its infusing thought HEALTH IS WEALTH through our proficient products. Covid-19 has helped us see the world with a new lens. It took us global pandemic to realise the importance of life and what truly matters in it. Nature has its own healing powers.
